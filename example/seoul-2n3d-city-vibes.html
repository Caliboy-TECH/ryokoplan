Why this structure works

The point is not to do everything. It is to make the city feel coherent.

  • Travel time can look short on a map but still feel draining if you jump neighborhoods too often.
  • Plan one late start after a nightlife-heavy evening—Seoul rewards that flexibility.
  • Use river walks, palace grounds, or quieter café districts to balance high-energy zones.

Before you customize it

Use these as your baseline decisions.

  • Avoid crossing the city too many times in one day.
  • Build coffee and rest stops into the route on purpose.
  • Subway is excellent, but short taxi hops can save energy at night.
