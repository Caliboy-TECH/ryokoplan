Privacy

A minimal privacy note for Ryokoplan before public launch.

What Ryokoplan stores

Saved routes, recent trips, and shared-trip history are primarily kept in your browser through local storage unless a separate backend feature says otherwise.

Shared links

When you open or share a trip link, the route data can be encoded into the URL so the trip can be reopened. Do not share links that contain private personal details.

Analytics and errors

Ryokoplan may later use lightweight analytics or error logging to improve stability, route quality, and usability. This page should be updated if that changes.

Contact

If you need a route or shared link removed from a public page you control, contact Ryokoplan through the contact page.
